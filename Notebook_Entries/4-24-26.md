## Entry 5: Final Demo Risk Review

**Date:** 4/24/26  

**Objective:** Identify the highest-risk integration issues remaining before the final demo.

**Work Completed:**  
After the mock demo and presentation preparation, the team reviewed what still needed to work reliably for the final demonstration. The main risks were reliable pump delivery, load-cell calibration, communication between handheld and homebase, Blynk connectivity, clear demo steps, and concentration verification with the UV/photodiode sensor. We also discussed fallback options, including using the serial monitor as a backup interface if Blynk or wireless communication became unreliable.

**Results / Decisions:**  
The team decided that the final demo needed a controlled sequence instead of an open-ended test. The demo should show puff input, algorithm calculation, app/chatbot feedback, pump delivery, and safety behavior in a repeatable order. The team also recognized that fallback paths were important because wireless systems and hardware integration could fail under demo conditions.

**Next Step:**  
Prepare a final demo script, continue hardware debugging, and make sure each subsystem has a working backup test.
