## Entry 4: Final Presentation and Feedback

**Date:** 4/30/26  

**Objective:** Present the final FadeX project and receive feedback from course staff.

**Work Completed:**  
The team delivered the final presentation to explain the project motivation, design, implementation, testing, and conclusions. The presentation described the shift from the original full vape concept to a safer homebase-centered prototype using pumps, load cells, ESP32 control, and app/serial interaction. We explained the adaptive algorithm, including how puff behavior and user state affected concentration decisions. We also discussed safety measures such as using a surrogate instead of nicotine, load-cell verification, pump limits, and emergency stop behavior.

**Results / Decisions:**  
The final presentation helped clarify the completed design and the remaining limitations. The team showed that FadeX was not just a mechanical mixing system but an adaptive control system that connected user behavior to physical dosing. Feedback from the presentation helped guide what needed to be emphasized in the final paper, especially the actual implemented design, verification results, and future work.
