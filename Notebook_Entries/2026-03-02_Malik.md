## 1. Algorithm Design & Math Models
*Documenting the dilution kinetics for the Home Base ESP32.*
To calculate the required volume of the 5.0% nicotine stock solution, I implemented the following standard volumetric formula:
$$V_{stock} = \frac{C_{target} \cdot V_{total}}{C_{stock}}$$
The diluent volume is then calculated as:
$$V_{diluent} = V_{total} - V_{stock}$$
