## Entry 4: Safety, Ethics, and Proposal Submission

**Date:** 2/12/26  

**Objective:** Complete the safety, ethics, and societal impact portion of the project proposal and submit the full proposal.

**Work Completed:**  
The team completed the proposal by documenting safety concerns related to nicotine, high-temperature heating elements, battery-powered electronics, and wireless control. Since nicotine is toxic and addictive, the proposal stated that nicotine would not be used for the initial course demonstration unless proper safety conditions were met. The team also referenced relevant standards and ethical concerns, including UL 8139 for vaping-device electrical safety and the IEEE Code of Ethics regarding public safety. The societal impact section described how FadeX could support nicotine cessation by reducing concentration gradually while preserving the user’s behavioral routine.

**Results / Decisions:**  
The completed proposal established the first official version of the FadeX design. At this stage, the project was still framed as a Bluetooth-enabled tapering vape with dual reservoirs, active pumping, app control, puff tracking, and firmware safety limits. The proposal also created the first documented set of references for nicotine addiction, gradual cessation, ESP32 hardware, pump hardware, battery charging, MOSFET control, and pressure sensing.

**Next Step:**  
Use proposal feedback to refine the design, prepare for team contract work, and begin moving from concept-level design into PCB planning and component selection.
