## Entry 4: PCB Review Preparation

**Date:** 2/18/26  

**Objective:** Prepare for PCB review by identifying the circuits and design questions that needed feedback.

**Work Completed:**  
The team prepared for PCB review by reviewing which circuits would likely be needed for the FadeX hardware. These included ESP32 control circuitry, pump drivers, power regulation, sensor inputs, and possible heating or detection circuits. We also discussed which parts were still uncertain and which design choices needed TA or mentor feedback before moving forward. Particular attention was given to pump selection, power requirements, and how the system would safely interface sensors and actuators with the ESP32.

**Results / Decisions:**  
The team identified that the PCB design should stay modular so that individual subsystems could be tested separately. This was important because the project had several high-risk areas, including fluid control, sensing, and power delivery. The PCB review was treated as an opportunity to catch design problems early before ordering boards.

**Next Step:**  
Use PCB review feedback to refine the schematic, update the BOM, and continue preparing for the design document.
