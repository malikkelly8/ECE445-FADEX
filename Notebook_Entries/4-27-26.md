## Entry 1: Final Demo Setup and System Readiness

**Date:** 4/27/26  

**Objective:** Prepare the FadeX prototype for final demonstration and verify that the most important subsystems were ready to show.

**Work Completed:**  
The team reviewed the final demo setup and checked which subsystems could be demonstrated reliably. The focus was on showing the homebase control system, pump delivery behavior, load-cell feedback, app or serial interaction, and the adaptive algorithm. Since the project had changed from the original fully integrated vape concept into a safer homebase-centered demonstration, the final setup emphasized controlled fluid mixing, puff-data input, and algorithm-driven concentration adjustment. We also reviewed backup plans in case Blynk connectivity, ESP-NOW communication, or pump/load-cell integration failed during the demo.

**Results / Decisions:**  
The team decided that the final demo should prioritize reliability and clarity. The most important story was that FadeX could collect usage information, calculate a next concentration, and physically dose liquid using feedback rather than blind timing alone. The demo sequence was kept controlled so that the evaluators could clearly see each major subsystem contribute to the final result.

**Next Step:**  
Run final checks on the ESP32 firmware, pumps, load cells, communication path, and user-facing interface before presenting.
