## Entry 3: Team Contract and Role Planning

**Date:** 2/16/26  
**Objective:** Complete the team contract and clarify responsibilities for the next design phase.

**Work Completed:**  
The team worked on planning responsibilities for the upcoming design and PCB stages. Since the proposal had defined the major subsystems, the next step was assigning ownership for hardware, electronics, software, and documentation tasks. The team contract was used to establish expectations for communication, meeting frequency, task delegation, and conflict resolution. We also discussed the need to balance workload across team members while still making progress toward PCB review and future demos.

**Results / Decisions:**  
The team contract helped turn the project from a concept into an organized engineering workflow. We agreed that the team needed a shared schedule, clearer task ownership, and regular progress tracking. The main near-term priorities were component selection, PCB planning, hardware feasibility, and early software architecture.

**Next Step:**  
Submit the team contract and begin preparing for PCB review by identifying required circuits, pin assignments, and component choices.
