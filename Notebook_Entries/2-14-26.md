## Entry 1: Component Sourcing and Parts List Planning

**Date:** 2/14/26  

**Objective:** Begin turning the proposal design into a concrete parts list so that long-lead components could be identified early.

**Work Completed:**  
The team discussed the need to create a more specific parts list based on the project proposal. The proposal described pumps, drivers, coils, sensors, and power electronics at a high level, but many exact part numbers still needed to be selected. A major focus was identifying precise micro-pumps, pump drivers, heating components, and power-conversion hardware that could support the FadeX design. The team also discussed the tradeoff between cost and precision, since some medical-style or high-accuracy components were significantly more expensive but could reduce technical risk.

**Results / Decisions:**  
The team decided that component sourcing needed to happen quickly because several parts could take weeks to arrive. We also identified that the pump and power systems were likely to be major design risks because accurate liquid dosing and reliable power delivery were central to the project. Cost was discussed as a design tradeoff: expensive parts could be justified only if they directly improved accuracy, safety, or reliability.

**Next Step:**  
Create a detailed BOM from the proposal and send the most important component choices to Shrey for review and possible ordering.
