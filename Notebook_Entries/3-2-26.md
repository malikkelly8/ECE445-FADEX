## Entry 1: Design Review Preparation

**Date:** 3/2/26  

**Objective:** Prepare to present the FadeX design document and explain the system architecture, requirements, and verification plan during design review.

**Work Completed:**  
The team reviewed the completed design document and focused on explaining the major design choices clearly. The most important design decision was the two-part architecture: a Home Base mixing station and a Handheld Vaporizer. We prepared to explain how the Home Base would handle the more complex fluidic system, including reservoirs, pumps, load cells, and concentration sensing, while the handheld device would preserve the user’s normal vaping behavior and collect puff data. We also reviewed the high-level requirements for mixing accuracy, adaptive tapering, and wireless communication.

**Results / Decisions:**  
The team decided that the design review should emphasize feasibility, safety, and verification. Instead of only describing the final product idea, we needed to show how each subsystem could be tested independently. The Home Base was framed as the main control and safety platform, while the handheld was framed as the user-facing behavior-tracking device.

**Next Step:**  
Present the design to the instructor and TAs, collect feedback, and revise subsystem plans based on the review.
