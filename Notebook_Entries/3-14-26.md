## Entry 4: Spring Break Work Planning

**Date:** 3/14/26 

**Objective:** Plan work that could be completed during or around spring break to keep the project moving.

**Work Completed:**  
The team discussed how to continue making progress despite the upcoming spring break. We reviewed the need to push forward on PCB design, parts ordering, software skeleton code, and mechanical planning. The team also discussed whether some work could be done remotely, such as algorithm development, app planning, documentation, and schematic review, while physical assembly and testing would depend on part availability.

**Results / Decisions:**  
The team decided that spring break should not pause the project completely. Software, documentation, and planning tasks could continue even if physical hardware work slowed down. This was important because final integration would require the hardware and software pieces to be ready at roughly the same time.

**Next Step:**  
Continue software planning, follow up on parts and chemical ordering, and prepare for hardware bring-up after spring break.
